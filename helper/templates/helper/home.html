How to tell if you need a battery jump start service

1) Your engine cranks (makes a clicking noise), but it doesn't start.

2) Your engine doesn't crank (no clicking noise) or start, and the lights don't come on.

3) One day it starts fine, then the next day it won't start at all.

4) You left the lights on over night.

5) Excessive heat or cold weather can weaken your battery. Hot temperatures can be worse for your car than cold weather.
